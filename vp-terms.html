About the App

Voice Pulse is an AI-powered personal coaching and psychological analysis tool. It analyzes the dynamics between people involved in conversations, identifies behavioral and communication patterns, and provides actionable recommendations for improving relationships and interpersonal communication — acting as a coach and psychologist at hand.
