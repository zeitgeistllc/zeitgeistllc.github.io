Sky Shield Defense

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Sky Shield Defense is developed and published by the app developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Game.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The Game operates primarily offline with on-device storage.

Local Data: Game progress, scores, upgrade levels, and preferences are saved locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

a. Apple Game Center: If you sign in, Apple may collect data per their privacy policy.

b. Apple StoreKit In-App Purchases: Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

c. Advertising - Google AdMob: When ads are enabled, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out via your device's privacy settings.

4. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5+, we request your permission before tracking. You may deny this and still use the Game.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally. Uninstalling the Game removes all associated data. We do not maintain any server-side user data.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our systems.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions, contact us at support@skyshieldgame.com
