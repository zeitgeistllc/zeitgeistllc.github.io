Voice Pulse

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Thank you for using Voice Pulse. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains how Voice Pulse handles your data.

On-Device Processing

Voice Pulse is designed with privacy at its core. All voice input, transcribed text, and psychological analysis are processed entirely on your device using its built-in AI capabilities. No audio, text, or analysis results are ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Microphone Access

Voice Pulse may request access to your device’s microphone to capture spoken conversations for analysis. Microphone access is used solely for this purpose. Voice recordings are processed locally in real time and are not stored, uploaded, or shared. You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Voice recordings or audio data
  • Transcribed text or conversation content
  • Psychological analysis results or recommendations
  • Personal identifiers or usage analytics

Data Storage

Any data you choose to save within the app (such as session notes or recommendations) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is fully under your control.

Third-Party Services

Voice Pulse does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. The app operates entirely offline for all core functionality.

Children’s Privacy

Voice Pulse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Support page.
